1. Simon Lizotte
PDGA# 8332

Simon Lizotte is a long-time pro tour player who moved from Germany to the United States when he was sixteen years old, to pursue competitive disc golf. Very quickly, he became a fan favorite as a flashy player who is not afraid to take risks out on the course. Simon Lizotte currently holds the world record for the world's fastest disc throw at 89.5 miles per hour.

2. Eagle McMahon
PDGA# 37817

Aptly named, Eagle has been shooting low scores since his entrance to the sport. Long ago Simon and Eagle were both signed by Discmania, where they took the tour by storm. They were quickly named "The Crushboys" by fans, as they both had monster drives. Eagle is another fan favorite similar to Simon in his ability to think outside the box, and use his excess of power for creative shots that stun the crowds and competition

3. Paul McBeth
PDGA# 27523

Paul "the beast" McBeth, has been making the game look simple for a very long time. With an intelligent game plan, well-rounded throwing ability, and lights-out putting, you can never count the six-time world champion out until the very end. Aside from winning the World Championship in six different years, Paul Macbeth is also known for shooting multiple "perfect" (18 under) rounds. He is truly a legend in the sport.
