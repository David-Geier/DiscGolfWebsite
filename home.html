Welcome, to Disc Golf

"Traditional golf is a good walk spoiled; disc golf is a good walk improved." - Source Unknown

Disc golf, much like ball golf, is a sport of extreme concentration and focus. It requires great patience, as it is inherently frustrating, but it gets me off my computer and out of the house, hot or cold, rain or shine.

Hi, I'm David Geier, and I love disc golf. My favorite part is how much thinking goes into each and every shot. Being both a wing and a gyroscope, spin and speed are both proportionally necessary to achieving the desired distance and shot shape. This is heavily influenced by form. Smooth is far, and developing consistent technique takes a lot of repetition, observation, and careful consideration. I also love that nearly anyone can play it. The community is also extremely positive across the board, especially for newer players.
